title: "collecting-document"
---

Events that belong to the category `collecting_document`.

|  |
| --- |
| A collecting document (<strong>cash-in</strong>) was successfully received and registered in the system. |
| A collecting document (<strong>cash-in</strong>) was received but could not be processed and was refused. |
