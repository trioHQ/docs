---
title: "Support"
---

Bugs should be reported using the messaging widget available from your Trio internet banking app, through our support portal at [suporte.trio.com.br](http://suporte.trio.com.br), or by sending an email to [suporte@trio.com.br](mailto:suporte@trio.com.br).

In order to get the best help possible, please provide as much context as possible about how you encountered the bug.

After sending your request, a member of our team will contact you as soon as possible.
