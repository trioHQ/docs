## **How to navigate the documentation**

Trio's documentation is organized into **three main sections**, designed to facilitate your integration journey - from introduction to full implementation:

### **1. Getting Started**

<Card title="Start here" icon="rocket" horizontal href="/quickstart">
  Follow our three step quickstart guide.
</Card>

> An introductory section, ideal for those just starting out.

Here you'll find an **overview of how the API works**, instructions for **setting up your environment**, **generating credentials** and **making your first requests**.

---

### **2. Developers**

> A technical and conceptual guide for developers.

In this part, you'll find **technical details**, **practical examples**, **good practices** and explanations about **entities, flows and authentication**.

It is the main material for those implementing integration with Trio.

---

### **3. API Reference**

> The complete technical catalog.

The **Reference** section presents **all the available endpoints**, with **examples of requests and responses**, as well as **parameters, data structures and error codes**.

It serves as a **quick reference source** during development.
